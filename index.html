Signage Products

Building/General Signage

Building and general signage are essential for businesses to create a strong, recognizable presence. These signs come in various forms such as outdoor building signage, directional signs, and internal branding. Custom-designed, these signs are weather-resistant for longevity.

Chromadek Signs

Chromadek signs are durable, weatherproof metal signs, often used for outdoor advertising and business signage. Made from galvanized steel with a painted finish, they offer a professional and cost-effective solution for large or small signs.

Correx Signs

Correx signs are lightweight, corrugated plastic signs, perfect for temporary advertising such as event signs and construction notices. They are affordable, easy to install, and practical for short-term use due to their portability.

Magnetic Signs

Magnetic signs are a portable and reusable solution, perfect for vehicles or metal surfaces. They can be easily removed or repositioned without causing damage, making them ideal for temporary advertising and business branding.

Perspex Plaques

Perspex plaques offer a sleek, modern look for offices, buildings, and corporate environments. These acrylic signs are available in engraved or printed formats, providing a polished and professional appearance.

Light Boxes

Light boxes are illuminated signs ideal for retail stores, airports, and shopping malls. Featuring backlit LED lighting, they ensure high visibility at all times, making them perfect for advertising and branding purposes.

Branding and Advertising Solutions

Vehicle Branding

Vehicle branding transforms cars, trucks, and fleets into mobile billboards. From full vehicle wraps to vinyl decals and magnetic signs, this service boosts brand visibility on the road, ensuring constant exposure.

Vinyl Stickers/Decals

Vinyl stickers and decals are adhesive graphics applied to surfaces such as windows, vehicles, and walls. They are perfect for branding, promotional materials, and creating eye-catching displays.

Vinyl Wall Art

Vinyl wall art offers a creative way to decorate or brand spaces with large, impactful designs. Whether for home, office, or retail environments, vinyl wall art adds an extra layer of personalization to any space.

Window Frosting

Window frosting enhances privacy while maintaining a modern aesthetic. This vinyl application is commonly used on office windows, conference rooms, and glass partitions, providing a sophisticated and elegant finish.

Contravision

Contravision is a perforated vinyl film that allows for one-way visibility, ideal for vehicle windows and storefronts. It provides privacy from the outside while displaying vivid graphics to the public.

Custom Fabrication and Materials

Perspex Laser Cutting

Perspex laser cutting offers precision and versatility, allowing for custom shapes and designs in acrylic materials. This service is commonly used for custom signage, display stands, and decorative elements.

Promotional Material and Graphic Design

Our graphic design and promotional services help businesses create eye-catching marketing materials. From flyers and brochures to social media graphics, we offer design solutions that enhance brand identity and reach.
